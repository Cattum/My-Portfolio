Signal Android

Signal Android is a 2D bullet hell strategy game. Players take on the role of a commander in the human resistance, using signal panels to control mechanical girls in battle. The ultimate goal is to break through the encirclement of robots and reclaim freedom for humanity.

Download Project Files

Project Introduction

Overview: The player will take on the role of a resistance tank driver, tasked with breaking through the encirclement built by the AI army and pushing forward. By controlling the tank through the cockpit panel, the player will gather parts, new modules, and other resources from battles to enhance their tank. Players will need to think creatively and strategically, using the collected resources to build a unique and powerful tank. Throughout this journey, the player will gradually uncover mysteries and get closer to the truth of the world.

Background: A century ago, mysterious anomalous spaces suddenly appeared around the world, where people discovered a new type of crystalline material, Substance A, with outstanding properties. Thanks to its excellent information transmission capabilities, superconductivity, and editability, Substance A greatly accelerated the development of data and mechanical sciences. A century later, human society has been completely taken over by an AI system called "The Subject," which manages and develops society in an orderly manner. However, human society still faces threats. An organization named B, claiming to "reclaim human dignity and freedom," is attempting to recreate anomalous spaces and disrupt the harmonious development of human society.
