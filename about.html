About Game

I envisions developing games as a profound art form to explore societal development, aiming to inspire global audiences to engage with modern society from diverse perspectives. By creating impactful game systems akin to literature and art, they hope to spark a modern Renaissance, counteract the trend of shallow entertainment, and provide intellectual and emotional guidance for modern individuals. Their university research will focus on studying games in media, using case studies to demonstrate their potential as conduits for ideas and tools for advancing contemporary humanism.

Deep Insight

This work explores the concept of a modern Renaissance, where individuals detach from the fast pace of life to reassess their purpose and contribute to society. It investigates the development of human history and humanism, emphasizing that we are at a stage requiring a Renaissance, which can be achieved through an ideological revival. The study also examines how ideas were spread during the Renaissance via media like painting and literature, arguing that digital media can serve a similar role today. Additionally, the project focuses on portraying diverse individuals' lives as reflections of the collective human experience, aiming to inspire individual consciousness and address social philosophical issues. Through these combined game studies, the user seeks to demonstrate how games can be a powerful medium for spreading ideas.
